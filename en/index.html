Meet me!

Who am I?

I am a novice web developer. I started my work on web projects thanks to the opening and management of the server of "the hated game of childhood ". You will be able to know the name of that game during the talking with me directly.

Most of the code that I'm preparing and improving at nights is an effect of self-taught work and stubbornness against the pressure of the outside, that tries to dissuade me from my dreamed and beloved way of development, which is Web Dev, by necessity of earning or offers of future proffesions.

More passion

Apart from a passion for Web Development I also like to play the piano, drums and producing electronic music. Unfortunately, all this is associated with considerable costs. That's why I hope that in a few years I will be able to continue self-development in music area by expanding equipment that I need for this, also buying electronic drums and renovating my old piano. Thanks to that in the future, after coming back from my dreamed job I will sit to my MIDI controler with more possibilities to visualize my ideas.

What is my current priority?

In this moment the most important thing for me is to find a good internship or a junior position in the company. Through that I would like to learn new, useful things and improve my code to perfection constantly. I would be glad to work in a nice athmosphere with helpful and reliable mentor.
